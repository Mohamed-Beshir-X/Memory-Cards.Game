About the Game

Leaderboard System

The game includes a Leaderboard that highlights the top players.

Players are ranked from first place downwards based on having the fewest mistakes.

You can find the Leaderboard icon on the far right side of the interface.

The leaderboard is powered by Local Storage, so your results are automatically saved on your device.

Player Name Formatting

When you enter your name, it is automatically formatted so that:

The first letter is always capitalized.

All other letters are set to lowercase.

This ensures consistency and accuracy in the Leaderboard ranking.

Audio & Effects

Enjoy engaging background music that makes the game more exciting.

Sound effects respond to your moves, whether correct or incorrect.
